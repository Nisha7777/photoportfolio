ABOUT

I'm a software engineering student that has a passion for innovative design and creativity that loves capturing the world's most beautiful moments through photography.

Through using vibrant colours to evoke a dreamy feel I aim to record what I see in the world around me through a unique and personal perspective.

While growing up, my family heavily valued learning more about different cultures that existed outside of my home in Sydney, Australia, through travelling.
This, in addition to increasingly coming to terms with my own cultural identity, has made me feel incredibly passionate towards learning as much as I can about the different cultures of individuals.

I hope to capture what I personally appreciate or find magical about these places through presenting scenes from around the world.

When i'm not editing photos or doing software engineering related things you'll find me:

  • Enjoying dramas, music and graphic novels from Asia.
  • Slowly (but surely) trying to learn a new language.
  • Patting my dog.
