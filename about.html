ABOUT

I'm a software engineering student from Sydney, Australia that has a passion for innovative design and creativity. I love capturing the world's most beautiful moments through photography.

Through using vibrant colours to evoke a dreamy feel I record what I see in the world around me through a unique and personal perspective.

I am increasingly coming to terms with my own cultural identity and often travelled with my family while growing up. This has made me feel incredibly passionate towards learning as much as I can about different cultures.

I hope to capture what I personally appreciate and find magical about these places through presenting scenes from around the world.

When i'm not editing photos or doing software engineering related things you'll find me:

  • Enjoying dramas, music and graphic novels from Asia.
  • Slowly (but surely) trying to learn a new language.
  • Patting my dog.
